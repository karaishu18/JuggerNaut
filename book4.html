Amrutara Santana by Gopinath Mohanty

This novel has the distinction of being the first Odia novel to receive the National Sahitya Akademi Award in 1955.

The novel talks about the trials and tribulations experienced by members of the Kondh Tribe, one of Odisha’s oldest indigenous tribes.

The focus of many of Mohanty’s novels was tribal life and its different manifestations.

For this, he has been compared to Chinua Achebe, the famous Nigerian author.

The novel was translated into English by eminent scholars from Odisha and was published with the title, The Dynasty of the Immortals.
